# Keith Walking Floor – Brand Rules (Source of Truth)

## Colors
- Primary Navy: #00395D
- Secondary Blue: #0077B6
- Accent Red: #8B1D18
- Text: #2E2E2E
- Muted Text: #5F6B73
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Borders: #E5E7EB

## Typography
- Font: Lato (fallback: Helvetica/Arial/sans-serif)
- Body: 14–16px, readable line-height (1.5–1.7)

## Buttons
- Primary button: pill shape (border-radius: 9999px)
- Background: dark charcoal
- Text: white
- No flashy effects

## Layout
- Use 8px spacing increments (8/16/24/32/48)
- Clean, industrial, minimal

## Rules
- Do NOT hardcode colors or fonts in UI files.
- Always use tokens from brand/tokens.css.
- If unsure, choose the simplest, most readable option.
